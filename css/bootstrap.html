    Walking Tours and Heritage Trails

    Walking Tours in Goa Discover Goa's true nature beyond its beaches and nightlife with walking tours in Goa that provide a close-up look at the rich history, culture, and way of life of the locals. Goa has been long known for its beaches and party life, Goa has a rich history of architectural wonders and historical legends. Discover heritage residences, mansions in the Portuguese style, and churches that date back centuries, from the lively streets of Panjim to the historic places of Margao. We guarantee an authentic experience that brings the stories of Goa to life with our walking tours in Goa, which are led by informed locals. Our walking tours in Goa are all made to offer a different viewpoint on the state's rich cultural diversity. You will explore Goa through the eyes of native Goans, who will share personal tales and lesser-known facts with you along the trip, whether you are a history buff or just a curious tourist. Discover Goa's colonial past and changing customs while exploring vibrant lanes and taking in exquisite architectural features. Through experiences that go beyond simple sightseeing, our excursions guarantee that you capture the essence of Goa and make connections and memories.  

    Village Excursions

    Goan Village Tours Discover the peace and quiet of Goa's countryside with our village tours, where rural life showcases a genuine and calm aspect of India. You may establish a strong connection with the land, the culture, and the people of Goa by visiting these villages. There’s truly no better way to understand a place than by immersing yourself in the traditions and rhythms of local life. Experience Goa from a different angle as you travel through its Goa’s villages, which are teeming with simplicity and tranquility. Embrace the Authentic Charm of Rural Goa Through our Goan Village Tours Breathe in the calm ambiance of Goan villages, where life moves at a different pace and local customs still hold strong. Our tours offer a chance to learn about traditional Goan practices and the unique celebrations that define village life. Wander through lush landscapes, marvel at the scenic beauty, and indulge in regional dishes prepared with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. This journey through Goan villages promises a truly enriching experience, filled with insights and lasting memories.  

    Culinary Experiences and Food Trails

    Goan Food Tours: A Culinary Journey through Goan Cuisine Goa’s cuisine is a memorable blend of taste and culture, with a touch of both Portuguese and regional flavors. Although many tourists opt for the traditional "fish curry and rice," Goan cuisine has much more to offer than that. Goan cuisine, which includes savory appetizers, sweets, and fiery curries, is a reflection of centuries of ethnic mingling. We offer food tours that go beneath the surface to uncover secret flavors and stories or hands-on cooking demonstrations are the finest ways to explore this vibrant culinary scene. Discover the unique ingredients and traditional techniques that define Goan cuisine. Every dish, from the layered sweetness of bebinca to the fiery notes of vindaloo, tells a story of Goa’s cultural heritage. Through our food tours, led by local culinary experts, you'll explore Goa’s diverse food landscape and gain a deeper understanding of its culinary roots. These tours provide an insider’s look at Goan food culture, taking you on a flavorful journey that goes beyond mere tasting, offering a true connection to Goa’s soul through its food.  

    Nature and Wildlife

    Nature Trails and Wildlife Tours in Goa The state of Goa is blessed with an abundance of natural resources and anchors a rich biodiversity, offering a mesmerizing world of wildlife to explore. With numerous wildlife sanctuaries, mangroves, and forests, the captivating Western Ghats present a myriad of flora and fauna. Nature trails in Goa provide an immersive way to experience this natural beauty firsthand. Exploring wildlife in Gao can be an exciting adventure for nature and wildlife enthusiasts. In fact it’s a heaven for bird watching. The boat ride, sunrise views, sunset points, ferry rides, and natural pools are fun surprises while nature trails. Our wildlife tours are intricately designed to showcase the best of Goa's nature trails. Whether you're gliding along calm rivers or strolling through deep forests, these nature trails in Goa offer a unique opportunity to get up close and personal with the diverse environment. Experience the magic of Goa through our carefully curated nature trails, where each path reveals new surprises and unforgettable encounters with the region's stunning scenery and varied fauna. Join us on these nature trails to fully immerse yourself in the breathtaking natural surroundings that Goa has to offer.  

    North Goa Trails

    Things to do in North Goa Even while Goa's beaches are unquestionably fascinating, North Goa has a wealth of experiences that are just waiting to be discovered. North Goa is full of unusual experiences that take you beyond partying and beach moods and bring you closer to Goa's true beauty.  Beyond its nightlife, North Goa's genuine charm is revealed in its cultural legacy and rich history. Those who are ready to seek beyond the familiar can discover North Goa's rich heritage through island tours, nature trails, and heritage walks. Immerse yourself in North Goa's distinctive experiences by exploring the serene island of Divar, admiring the scenic trails of Chorao, meandering through the vibrant lanes of Fontainhas, or seeing the historic sights of Old Goa. These remarkable events are only a taste of what is to come. Try a treasure hunt, a mud-bathing ritual, or a vibrant bar-hopping trail for a genuinely unique experience. These are excellent ways to discover North Goa outside of the usual tourist destinations. Every traveler can find something remarkable in North Goa, whether they are looking for adventure, culture, or peace and quiet.    

    South Goa Trails

    Things to do in South Goa Combining unspoiled natural beauty with a rich cultural legacy, South Goa provides a tranquil haven from the hustle and bustle. From pristine waterfalls and prehistoric rock sculptures to secret caverns and picturesque nature paths, it's a sanctuary for people looking for serene yet engrossing experiences. The region's distinct appeal is displayed through waterfall hopping, touring spice plantations, and stopping by local bakeries. While peaceful locations along the route offer fascinating flora and animals, village visits offer a window into Goan culture. Ideal for a day excursion, South Goa's unique experiences entice visitors to explore its undiscovered treasures, making it a dream destination for those seeking to experience Goa's more sedate side. Beyond its natural splendor, South Goa provides an exploration of Goan history and culture. You can observe traditional farming methods and sample the flavors that characterize Goan cuisine by visiting nearby spice plantations. Ancient rock carvings and enigmatic caverns connect tourists to the tales of the past while introducing a dash of adventure and historical intrigue. With each pastry, traditional bakeries—many of which have been family-run for generations—bring Goa's legacy to life. The experiences offered by South Goa combine relaxation and exploration, making it the perfect destination for tourists looking for peace and genuine adventures. You can stroll along verdant nature trails, swim beneath remote waterfalls, or discover local customs.

    The Soul Travelling Family

    Varun Hegde

    • Varun Hegde
    • Co-Founder

    It’s the man himself, the first Soul Traveller. Varun tells us about what inspired him to start Soul Travelling and the road ahead. “Can't believe it's been a year already. I still remember that day on 12th November. The first ever Margao City Walk. I'd put in a lot of effort for curating it, but went easy on the promotions. Only 3 people turned up, but it was a lot of fun showing them around Margao. In the second walk, we focused more on promotions - and we had 26 people! This time we had to learn - about managing crowd. It's been a delightful journey since then. Born and brought up in Goa, it has a special place in my heart. I did my engineering, and then worked for 4 long years in the IT industry. My last job was in Frankfurt. I had an amazing time travelling the lengths and breaths of Europe, and realized that travel was a part of life in the European culture. People there don’t just travel for sightseeing. Exploring new cultures, traditions, cuisines, meeting new people are some of the reasons that drive European travellers. That is where the idea of Soul Travelling was born. In Goa, you have a very interesting Baga-Calangute model. You are bombarded with questions the moment you say you're from Goa - the stereotypes exist. So the time is ripe to break the stereotypes and provide an alternate model for Tourism - and that is what Soul Travelling intends to do - Change the way people perceive travel in Goa and rest of India.”
